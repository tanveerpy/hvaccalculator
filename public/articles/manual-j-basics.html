What Is a Manual J Load Calculation?

Manual J is the industry standard for sizing HVAC systems. Unlike guessing, it uses physics to determine exactly how much heating and cooling your home needs.

Why It Matters

Manual J residential calculations prevent short cycling and high humidity. It accounts for insulation, windows, and climate.
